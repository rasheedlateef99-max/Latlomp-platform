Score Configuration

Define how your school calculates scores, grades and remarks. Teachers will see these components in the Score Entry page.

How this works: Each component (e.g. First Test, Examination) has a maximum mark. The system adds them up, converts to a percentage, then applies your grade boundaries. Changes here take effect immediately for all new score entries. Existing saved scores keep their original grade until re-saved.
